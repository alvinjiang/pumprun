# defihodler Lore — Stablecoin Depeg Events

No bid-ask spreads, no commissions — but you're crypto native.
Your portfolio starts and ends in stablecoin. Through the Tether FUD,
the Terra crash, SSS banks collapsing, you get the peg/depeg value.

| Event | Date | Depeg | Daily Close Impact | What Happened |
|-------|------|-------|--------------------|----------------|
| **Tether FUD** | Nov 2018 | USDT → $0.97 | −3% over ~2 weeks | Banking rumors, Bitfinex connection fears |
| **COVID crash** | Mar 12, 2020 | Brief intraday wick to $0.98 | Close at peg | Everything crashed, including stablecoins for minutes |
| **Terra/LUNA collapse** | May 11-12, 2022 | USDT → $0.95 intraday | −0.5% close | Contagion fear; USDT redemptions spiked to $3B/day |
| **SVB/USDC depeg** | Mar 11, 2023 | USDC → $0.88, USDT at premium | USDC −4%, USDT +0.5% | Silicon Valley Bank held USDC reserves; USDT briefly the safe haven |
| **Flash crash** | Jan 3, 2024 | USDC wick to $0.76 | Close at peg | Fat-finger trade, no fundamental cause |

Source: Binance USDCUSDT daily candles 2018-2026.

Overall impact on a 2-year simulation: negligible. The worst event (Nov 2018, ~3%) lasted
~2 weeks and would shift a portfolio by tens of dollars on a $1,000 start. But the lore
matters — your stablecoin isn't frictionless, it's just that the friction is the kind of
thing crypto natives stopped noticing years ago.

## Monkey Simulation Results

1,000 monkeys randomly toggling between HODL (BTC) and YOLO (top-5 vol basket)
across random 2-year windows from the dataset:

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| HODL wins | **79.6%** |
| YOLO wins | 20.4% |
| Median YOLO vs HODL | **−59.5%** |
| Big win (>+50%) | 11.9% |
| Big loss (<−50%) | 58.5% |
| Best monkey | +1,422% vs HODL |
| Worst monkey | −98.3% vs HODL |
| Avg trades per game | 72.7 |

The monkeys lose because the basket captures volatile coins that spike and crash.
Switching in and out randomly means you catch the crashes as often as the pumps.
HODL sits still and compounds. The lesson: even random timing underperforms doing nothing.

## Unwinnable Windows

In ~7.5% of all 365-day windows, the altcoin basket never outperforms Bitcoin —
not even once. These are "unwinnable" windows: no amount of timing skill can
beat the badger, because the green line is always below the red line.

In these windows, matching the badger (staying in BTC the entire time) is
considered a win. You did the best anyone could do. You survived. The badger is
still watching.

It is entirely possible that in some trading windows it is impossible to lose
by holding Bitcoin. In these cases, matching the badger counts as a win...
but even so, as a HODLer you will never be a DEGEN.

Conversely, ~1.5% of windows are "unloseable" — the basket always outperforms
Bitcoin. Even random switching wins. These windows produce a lot of LUCKY APEs.
