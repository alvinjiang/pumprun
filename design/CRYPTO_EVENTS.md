## Season Tags (for game reveal)

These contextual tags appear during the end-game reveal, describing the
era the player's 365-day window fell within:

- **ICO Summer** (2017): Whitepapers, promises, and 10,000% gains in a week
- **Crypto Winter** (2018–2019): Everything down 80%. Builders stayed. Tourists left.
- **Crypto Spring** (mid-2019): Libra. Bakkt. Cautious optimism. Didn't last.
- **COVID Crash** (Mar 2020): Everything dies. Everything reboots.
- **DeFi Summer** (mid-2020): Yield farming, food tokens, liquidity mining. Degens unite.
- **Institutional Wave** (Q4 2020–Q1 2021): Wall Street buys. Mainstream arrives.
- **NFT Mania** (Q1 2021): JPEGs for millions. The metaverse is coming. Apparently.
- **China Ban & Recovery** (mid-2021): Mining exodus. BTC hashrate drops, then roars back.
- **Metaverse Rotation** (Q4 2021): MANA, SAND, GALA. Virtual land at Manhattan prices.
- **LUNA Collapse** (May 2022): Death spiral. $40B gone. Contagion spreads.
- **Crypto Winter II** (mid-2022): 3AC, Celsius, Voyager, FTX. Bodies everywhere.
- **ETF Era** (2024): Wall Street arrives. Again. This time with prospectuses.
- **Meme Coin Spring** (Mar 2024): WIF, PEPE, BONK. Dogs, frogs, and hats.
- **Post-Halving Rally** (Q4 2024): BTC to $100K. The cycle plays out on schedule.
- **Quiet Bull** (2025): Less volatility. More institutions. Bitcoin is boring now.
